Cat Insurance

Our cat insurance plans offer comprehensive coverage for your feline friends. Whether it's accidents, illnesses, or routine care, our plans are tailored to meet your cat's specific needs.

What does Cat Insurance cover?

Depending on which cover you choose, Cat Insurance can offer you up to 80% of eligible vet costs back if your cat suffers a Specified Accidental Injury or illness. You’ll also have cover for home vet visits, emergency boarding, and access to optional cover for routines such as desexing and teeth cleaning.

Key Coverage Benefits:

Accident and injury coverage

Illness coverage

Emergency medical care

Routine check-ups and vaccinations

Top 5 things to know about cat insurance

Cover for felines of all ages
Cats can be insured from six weeks into their senior years.

Insurance for the unexpected and more
Pet insurance for cats offers coverage for accidental injuries and illnesses, plus routine care as an optional extra.

Protect your cat for life
You can cover your feline for life if they’ve been insured before age nine and there are no breaks in cover.

Get reimbursed for some or all expenses
Pet insurance can cover 70-90% of your eligible vet bills – up to an annual limit.

Affordable cover
Cats are typically cheaper to insure than dogs.
