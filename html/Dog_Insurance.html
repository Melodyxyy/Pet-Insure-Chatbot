Dog Insurance

Our dog insurance plans provide comprehensive coverage for your canine companions. Whether it's accidents, illnesses, or routine check-ups, our plans are designed to give you peace of mind.

What does Dog Insurance cover?

Dog Insurance offers cover for up to 80% of eligible vet costs associated with your best mate’s specified accidental injuries, and for costs associated with illness. You’ll also have cover for emergency boarding, and access to optional cover for routines such as tick control and obedience training.

Key Coverage Benefits:

Accident and injury coverage

Illness coverage

Emergency medical care

Routine check-ups and vaccinations
